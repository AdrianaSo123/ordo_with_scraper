# Sprint 20: Efficiency Polish

## Goal

Final density and accessibility refinements for power users.

## Features

- **Density Toggle**: Toggle between "Reader" (Relaxed) and "Developer" (Compact) views.
- **TTS Toggle Shortcut**: `Cmd + Shift + P` to toggle audio narration.
- **History Search**: `Cmd + F` for full-text search within the chat history.

## Success Criteria

- Switching to "Compact" reduces margins and padding significantly.
- High-speed history search works instantly.
